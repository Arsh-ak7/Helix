# Helix

Helix is a modern fitness tracking application for iOS, designed to help you monitor your workouts and achieve your fitness goals.

## Features

* **Workout Tracking:** Easily log your exercises, sets, and reps.
* **Exercise Library:** Browse a comprehensive library of exercises to build your workout routines.
* **Workout History:** Keep a detailed journal of your past workouts to track your progress over time.
* **HealthKit Integration:** Seamlessly sync your workout data with Apple Health.
